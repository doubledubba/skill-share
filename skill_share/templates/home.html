Everyone knows things. This is a given. But wouldn't it be great if that knowledge was shareable, instead of hoarded? SkillShare is a venue for crowdsourcing both your education and projects. It helps you find people who know things that you don't. You can reach out to them to learn a new skill yourself, or commission them for a project. The relationship works both ways; you go to others for what they know, and they will come to you for the skills you are willing to share.
